Travel

Serendipitous Blaggagio is the name I give to my recent and ongoing travels. It started with Operation Serendipitous Blaggagio in November 2011 - an attempt at hitchhiking across the Atlantic and then exploring Latin America. I didn't get a lift, but I would still like to cross, so if you're looking for crew, or know someone who is, please find out more about my sailing experience and plan. For now, the plan is to cycle in Europe.

Blog posts covering the adventure are tagged "serbla".

See also photos on flickr, and my twitter account for updates.

Code
