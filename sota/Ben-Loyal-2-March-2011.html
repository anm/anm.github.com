SOTA Activation: Ben Loyal, GM/NS-052

2 March 2011

Alistair MacLeod, MM0HAI

A brief report on a brief activation

Ben Loyal is FIXME: one of / the? most northerly mountains in Scotland. It's near Tongue. Loyal means level, which refers to the summit plateau. There are three main peaks; the highest and SOTA qualifying one is .... ...

The summit is a large slab. It was wet. A strong wind and mist made it more exciting.

Despite the remote location two contacts were made on 2m with stations in Stornoway and Elgin. I managed to post a spot by SMS but there was no response - I probabably did not wait long enough. It was too cold.
